# Decision Log Entry (DAR / ADR)

Add **new** entries to `docs/decisions.md` **at the top** (most recent first).  
Use **either** classic **ADR** sections (*Context / Decision / Consequences*) **or** the structured block below.

---

## Structured format (copy below this line)

**Decision:** &lt;short title&gt;

### Context

Why this decision is needed. What broke or what ambiguity exists.

### Options Considered

- Option A — pros / cons  
- Option B — pros / cons  

### Decision

What we chose and why.

### Status

`Pending` | `Approved` | `Rejected`

### Consequences

- What becomes easier  
- What becomes harder  
- Follow-up work  

---

## ADR format (alternative)

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Rejected | Superseded  
**Decided by:** &lt;role or name&gt;

### Decision
